Submit Your Business

Get your business listed on Bitcoin directories and maps.

Coinmap

Add your business to the world map of places that accept Bitcoin.

Visit Coinmap

Spendabit

List your products on a search engine for things you can buy with Bitcoin.

Visit Spendabit

UseBitcoins

Submit your business to a directory of merchants accepting Bitcoin.

Visit UseBitcoins

How to Submit

Each service above has its own submission process. Click the link for the service you'd like to use and follow their instructions to get your business listed.

Being listed on multiple services can help increase your visibility to Bitcoin users around the world.
