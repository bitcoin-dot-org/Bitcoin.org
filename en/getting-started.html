Submit Your Business

Get Your Business Listed

If you accept Bitcoin, you can get your business listed on several popular directories and services. This helps customers find you and increases your visibility in the Bitcoin ecosystem.

Business Directories

Coinmap

Coinmap is an interactive map that shows places where Bitcoin is accepted. Add your business to help customers find you in your area.

Visit Coinmap

Spendabit

Spendabit is a search engine for products you can buy with Bitcoin. List your store to reach more customers.

Visit Spendabit

useBitcoins

useBitcoins is a directory of businesses, websites, and services that accept Bitcoin.

Visit useBitcoins

How to Submit Your Business

Each directory has its own submission process. Generally, you will need to:

  1. Visit the directory's website
  2. Look for an "Add Business" or "Submit" link
  3. Fill out your business information, including name, address, and website
  4. Indicate that you accept Bitcoin as payment

Once submitted, your business will be reviewed and added to the directory.
