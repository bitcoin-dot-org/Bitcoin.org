Visibility

Accepting Bitcoin at your business is a great way to attract new customers. To get started, you simply need to display a QR code with your Bitcoin address somewhere around your checkout area. There are also many merchant solutions that make it easy to accept Bitcoin.

Submit your business to online directories to help customers spend bitcoins at your business.

Submit Your Business

Get your business listed on Bitcoin directories and reach more customers.

Coinmap

Coinmap is an interactive map showing places where you can pay with Bitcoin.

Visit Coinmap

Spendabit

Spendabit is a search engine for products that can be purchased with Bitcoin.

Visit Spendabit

UseBitcoins

UseBitcoins.info maintains a directory of businesses that accept Bitcoin.

Visit UseBitcoins

How to Submit Your Business

To get your business listed, visit one or more of the services above and follow their submission instructions. Each service has its own process for adding new businesses. Typically, you will need to provide your business name, address, and a description of the products or services you offer.
